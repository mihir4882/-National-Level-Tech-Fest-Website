EVENT DESCRIPTION:

Unleash the fabricator within you and design a Trussed Arch Bridge. Get as creative as possible and use Popsicle sticks and fevicol to create a bridge that can endure extreme loads without failure.

TEAM MEMBERS:

3 to 4 members per team.

EVENT FORMAT:

Round 1:

Every team has to design a trussed arch bridge using Popsicle sticks and fevicol as adhesive.
The bridge will be tested for strength by applying vertical loads on it.
The weight of the bridge will also be checked.


 

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA:

  • The final scoring will be on the basis of ratio of max load to weight of the bridge.

  • NOTE:  Decision of the judges and coordinators will be final and binding to all.
