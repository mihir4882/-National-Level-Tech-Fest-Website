The term ‘Industrial IoT’, though relatively new, is making its presence felt in the huge ocean of digital technologies. It brings together brilliant machines, advanced analytics and people at work, and is completely transforming the way we think of industries and machines, making it more connected and data intensive. Irrespective of whether it’s is used for predictive analysis of fault detection, optimising production lines or protecting your machines from external tamper, IIoT is creating a new avenue to drive businesses to maximum profitability.

 

When there is so much potential insight available to us out there, why not go the extra mile and hunt for it?

 

General Electric, in association with Pragyan, NIT Trichy presents the IoT Asset Performance Management workshop!

The workshops will be focussing on the following points of interest:

 

  1. Tenets of Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)

Duration: 1 hour

  • Introduction to IIoT

  • IoT vs IIoT

  • Case studies

 

  1. Cloud Computing in Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)

Duration: 1.5 hour

  • IaaS, SaaS based Application Performance Management and PaaS (Cloud Foundry, Predix).

  • Multilatency in SaaS Applications

  • Breaking the monoliths

  • Microservices

  • Big Data and Spark

 

  1. EDGE computing in Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)

Duration: 45 minutes

  • IT Connectors

  • OT Connectors

  • EDGE to cloud connectivity

  • EDGE analytics

 

  1. Cyber and Application Security

Duration: 45 minutes

  • Threats and challenges

  • Tools and technologies

 

Date: 2nd March 2018

1) Does the workshop have any prerequisites?

There are no prerequisites for the workshop. Any student interested in Electronics engineering and other related fields of engineering can attend the workshop.

 

2) How should the payment be made for the workshop?

Payment can be made online. Payment guidelines will be mailed to the participant’s mail-id once registered.

 

3) On what basis are workshop registrations confirmed?

Registrations are confirmed after payment on first come first serve basis. Confirmation of your participation is subject to availability.

 

4) Will the fees paid for the workshop be refunded in case I fail to attend the workshop?

Fees once paid, is strictly non-refundable. Requests for refund will not be encouraged.

 

5) Will accommodation be provided?

Yes. We provide accommodation depending on the availability and your place of residence. You will have to register separately for that. Stay tuned to the website for updates on accommodation.

 

6) Will certificates be provided?

Yes. A certificate from Pragyan, the ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 20121:2012 certified Techno-Management Festival of NIT, Trichy will be provided after the completion of the workshop.
