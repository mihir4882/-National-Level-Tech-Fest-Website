Have you ever thought of a product that can change the world but feel that you don't have the necessary skills to visualise and design it? Worry not, for this workshop conducted by Autodesk in association with Pragyan, NIT Trichy is all set to introduce you to Fusion 360, a powerful tool used for 3D designing, validating, fabricating, and simulating product designs and digital prototypes. Don't miss this opportunity of experiencing a hands-on tutorial that teaches you the life cycle of a product design!

To get started with Autodesk Fusion 360, read this document, "Autodesk Fusion 360".

Topics to be covered in this workshop are:

  • Introduction: Learn about the user interface and workspaces in Fusion.
  • Sketch: Use 2D sketches to create intelligent and predictable designs.
  • Sculpt: Create organic shapes using T-Splines.
  • Solid Modeling: Use history-based modeling techniques to create prismatic designs.
  • Manage and Collaborate: Create versions of a design and share your design work.
  • Rendering: Generate an image of your design with camera, lighting, texture, and shading.
  • Drawing Creation: Create 2D manufacturing drawings and generate PDF and DWG documentation.

1) Does the workshop have any prerequisites?

There are no prerequisites for the workshop. Any student interested can attend the workshop.

 

2) How should the payment be made for the workshop?

Payment can be made online. Payment guidelines will be mailed to the participant’s mail-id once registered.

 

3) On what basis are workshop registrations confirmed?

Registrations are confirmed after payment on first come first serve basis. Confirmation of your participation is subject to availability.

 

4) Will the fees paid for the workshop be refunded in case I fail to attend the workshop?

Fees once paid, is strictly non-refundable. Requests for refund will not be encouraged.

 

5) Will accommodation be provided?

Yes. We provide accommodation depending on the availability and your place of residence. You will have to register separately for that. Stay tuned to the website for updates on accommodation.

 

6) Will certificates be provided?

Yes. A certificate from Pragyan, the student-run ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 20121:2012 certified techno-managerial organisation of NIT Trichy will be provided after the completion of the workshop.
