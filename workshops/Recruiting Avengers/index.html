From the ultimate Marvel Cinematic Universe, be like Captain America and lead your team to success, Be like Iron Man and make your way out of any problem, be like Thor and find your true potential, be like Hulk and smash the obstacles in the way and make your way to the Aim.

Team Members:

"This event allows team participation with 3 members only. ".

Round 1: 'Avengers Assemble' :

  • In this round the participants will perform different task individually as per the marvel character chose by them from the given options. And combined score will be generated.

  • Round 2: The Avengers Initiative.

  • In this round, a chase sequence will be created in which, every clue will have a hidden avengers name in it and that character will be available at that checkpoint (But limited quant.) The participants need to prioritize characters as per their value and hidden messages. And collect as many as possible.

  • Round 3: The Infinity Wars.

  • We will create a storyline according to the fan theories, synopsis and Easter eggs. The participants have to take part in a real life infinity war sequence. Every spot they reach will give them a part of the original storyline up and clue to the next spot. At the end, using those messages and other criteria, they have to write their own storyline. The one most similar to original one will win.

Round 1:

  • Top 20 teams with most combined points will make their way to Round 2.

  • Round 2:

  • Top 10 teams with most character points will make their way to Round 3.

  • Round 3:

  • The Team with the most similar storyline with the original one and covers maximum criteria will win!
