Building Information Modeling (BIM) may sound trivial, but it is phenomenally changing the way on how buildings, infrastructure and utilities are planned, designed, built and managed. As BIM adoption continues to grow around the world, governments are promoting its ability to eliminate waste on public projects and even mandating its use as a part of construction sector reform, resulting in cost savings and climate-change mitigation strategies.

Autodesk, in association with Pragyan, NIT Trichy presents the Building Information Modeling workshop!

To learn more about Building Information Modelling, read this document, "About BIM".

Topics to be covered in this workshop are:

  • Introduction to Building Information Modeling
  • The ‘I’ in BIM
  • Introduction to Autodesk Revit Basics
  • Exploring the User Interface
  • Working with Revit Elements and Intro to Families
  • Starting a Project
  • Developing the Building Model
  • Detailing Your Design
  • Documenting Your Design
  • Viewing the Building Model
  • Using Dimensions
  • Creating Renderings
  • Multidisciplinary Coordination
  • Quick Overview (Demo/Video)
  • Modeling Structural Elements
  • Modeling Electrical Systems
  • Modeling Plumbing Systems
  • Modeling Mechanical Systems
  • Coordination and Interference Checking
  • Intro to Civil 3D and Infraworks 360
  • Introduction to A360
